Drawit

A multiplayer drawing game primarly developed in the summer of 2017. One player draws a word while the other players guess what is being drawn. The player with the most amount of correct guesses wins. The game also supports Freedraw, where a blank canvas is given so that multiple players can draw at the same time. Drawit uses a lobby based system for the gamemodes and supports password protection and invite links. You can play the game at this link.
